Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 pro

Intro

Ever wanted a phone that satisfies the needs of every type of phone user? Because this is the one. The Redmi Note 9 Pro is a killer handset that thins out the competition in the arena of budget smartphones with all the specs you would need on your smartphone and it comes at a price of 14,499 INR. This is definitely not surprising as almost every phone made by Xiaomi comes in this price tag or lower. But by far, this is the best phone in the market under the budget of 15k.
